HOW YOU CAN CONTRIBUTE.

We are searching Flickr for images containing the genus and species either as a 'machine tag' (eg. taxonomy;botanical="Eucalyptus camaldulensis") or appearing in the title of the image. For your photo to appear in the search results on the app, your photo must be licensed under Creative Commons and your privacy setting in Flickr must be set as "Safe". In the future we hope to allow users to link to images in other locations.
